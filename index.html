    About the Show

    Every year, we have exhibitors from around the area come to show their antique farm equipment, ranging from tractors and mowers to threshers and steam engines. There's also several activies to watch like blacksmithing, corn shelling, a running sawmill, binding, threshing, and so much more!

    Antique Tractors

    John Deere, Case IH, Ford, Allis Chalmers, and more!

    Blacksmithing

    Live blacksmiths working metal with anvils and forges!

    Threshing

    Live threshing demonstrations!

    Sawmill

    Watch the crew cut logs using the old sawmill!

    Flea Market

    A large flea market with antiques, tools, clothing, toys...they've got everything, really!

    Train Station

    Stop by one of the train stations to take a ride around the fairgrounds!
